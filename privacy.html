Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

SurfsUp is built with your privacy in mind. We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide surf conditions and keep the app running. We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

Information We Collect

Location Data

SurfsUp requests access to your device's location to:

  • Show beaches near you on the map
  • Determine whether it is daytime or nighttime at your location (for the Adaptive theme)
  • Fetch weather and surf conditions for nearby beaches

Your location is only used on-device and when making requests to Apple's WeatherKit and the open-meteo.com marine API. It is never stored on our servers, because we don't have any.

User Preferences

Your settings — including your name, skill level, wave size preference, unit system, wetsuit threshold, and favourite beaches — are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device unless you use iCloud backup, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

BeachBuddy AI

When you use BeachBuddy, your message and the current beach conditions (name, wave height, sea temperature, wind speed, surf score) are sent to Groq, Inc. to generate a response. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Groq's use of this data is governed by Groq's Privacy Policy.

Weather Data

Weather information is fetched using Apple WeatherKit. Apple may collect anonymised usage data as part of this service. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Marine Conditions Data

Wave height and sea surface temperature are fetched from open-meteo.com, a free and open marine weather API. Only your beach's coordinates are sent in these requests. No personal data is transmitted.

Purchases & Subscriptions

All purchases and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. SurfsUp never sees or stores your payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple Watch

If you use the SurfsUp Apple Watch companion app, your favourite beaches and current conditions are transferred from your iPhone to your Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This transfer happens entirely on your local devices and does not pass through any external server.

Analytics & Advertising

SurfsUp does not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Data Retention

All data SurfsUp stores lives on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. We do not maintain any server-side records tied to your identity.

Children's Privacy

SurfsUp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
